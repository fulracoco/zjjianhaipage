公司简介

深耕船舶动力发动机领域，以高品质、快交付、优服务为宗旨

镇江舰海船舶配件有限公司

Zhenjiang Jianhai Marine Parts Co., Ltd.

镇江舰海船舶配件有限公司是一家集研发、生产、销售、服务于一体的专业船舶制造、水上运输设备零配件销售的制造企业。公司深耕船舶动力发动机领域，依托专业技术团队、精密制造设备、严格质量管控与完善供应链体系，为国内外航运企业、造船厂、船舶维修厂、发动机主机厂提供原厂品质、高性价比的柴油机配件与解决方案。

Zhenjiang Jianhai Marine Parts Co., Ltd. is a professional shipbuilding and marine transportation equipment parts manufacturing enterprise that integrates R&D, production, sales, and services. Specializing in the field of marine power engines, the company leverages its technical team, precision manufacturing equipment, stringent quality control, and well-established supply chain system to provide domestic and international shipping companies, shipyards, marine repair facilities, and engine manufacturers with original-quality, high-value diesel engine parts and solutions.

主营产品包括淄柴、潍柴等主流船用发动机阀门，中速机阀门以及低速机阀门等。产品均通过ISO 9001质量管理体系认证，关键零部件经过严格的性能测试与疲劳试验，确保与原厂主机完美匹配。公司坚持以高品质、快交付、优服务为宗旨，为船舶企业、造船厂、船东及维修单位提供一站式动力配套解决方案。

Main Products: Marine engine valves for Zichai, Weichai and other mainstream brands, including medium-speed and low-speed engine valves. All products have passed ISO 9001 quality management system certification, with key components undergoing rigorous performance testing and fatigue trials to ensure perfect compatibility with original engine mainframes. The company adheres to the principles of high quality, fast delivery, and superior service, providing one-stop power solutions for shipbuilding enterprises, shipyards, shipowners, and maintenance units.

船用发动机进排气阀是柴油机配气机构的核心部件，主要负责控制气缸进气→压缩→做功→排气四个工作过程，直接影响发动机动力性、经济性与可靠性。公司产品采用高强度耐热合金精密锻造加工，具备优异的高温强度、抗氧化性、耐腐蚀性与耐磨性，可适应重油、轻油等多种燃料工况，产品与淄柴、潍柴、宁动、广柴、MAN等主流机型完全互换。

The intake and exhaust valves of marine engines are core components of the diesel engine's valve train, primarily responsible for controlling the four working processes of cylinder intake → compression → power → exhaust. They directly affect the engine's power output, fuel efficiency, and reliability. Our products are precision-forged from high-strength heat-resistant alloy, featuring excellent high-temperature strength, oxidation resistance, corrosion resistance, and wear resistance — capable of adapting to various fuel conditions such as heavy oil and light oil, with full interchangeability with Zichai, Weichai, Ningdong, Guangchai, MAN and other mainstream models.
